A choose-your-own-adventure web game built for the [Goose Advent of AI - Day 2 Challenge](https://github.com/block/goose/discussions/5949).

## Play

Simply open `index.html` in your browser!

## Features

- **11 unique endings** to discover
- **6+ choice points** with branching story paths
- **Character stats** (Courage, Wisdom, Kindness) that change based on your decisions
- **Falling snowflakes** animation
- **Beautiful winter styling** with glowing effects
- **Auto-save progress** with localStorage
- **Restart button** to play again
